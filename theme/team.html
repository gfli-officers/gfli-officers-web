Get to know our leadership board.

Fun Fact: She has over 700+ hours in Animal Crossing: New Horizons.

Co-Founder and Co-President

Diana is a 2nd year PhD student in the Harvard-MIT Health Sciences & Technology program who researches the intersection of neuroimaging, immunology, & technology. Her role within GFLI@MIT includes interfacing with external entities and GFLI advocacy.

Fun Fact: She attended 13 different public schools before completing high school.

Davy Deng

GFLI@MIT Co-Founder and Co-President

Davy is a 2nd year PhD Student in the Harvard-MIT Health Sciences & Technology program who is interested in the intersection of machine learning and cancer evolution. His role within GFLI@MIT includes account managing, fundraising, and financial affairs.
